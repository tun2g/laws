# Vietnamese ↔ English Legal Terminology Glossary

## How to use this glossary

1. Before translating, ctrl-search for the Vietnamese term you are about to translate.
2. Use the **standardized** column — do not improvise equivalents unless a term is truly absent.
3. On the first occurrence in a document, you may add the source-language term in parentheses: `People's Court (Tòa án nhân dân)`.
4. For terms in the **Keep original** section, never translate them — they are identifiers, not descriptions.

## 12. Terms to Keep in Original Language (Never Translate)

These identifiers and internationally recognized codes must appear unchanged in both Vietnamese and English documents.

### Law citations
Keep the full citation exactly as in the source:
- `Nghị định 01/2021/NĐ-CP` — do NOT render as "Decree 01/2021/ND-CP" in VI→EN (keep the exact accented Vietnamese abbreviation or use the full English title on first occurrence only)
- `Thông tư 47/2019/TT-BTC`
- `Luật số 59/2020/QH14`
- `Công văn 1234/BTP-PLDSKT`

**Practice**: On first occurrence in EN output, you may write: `Decree No. 01/2021/ND-CP dated January 4, 2021 (on Enterprise Registration)`. On all subsequent mentions: `Decree No. 01/2021/ND-CP`.

### Form codes and template codes
- `Mẫu số 01-ĐK-TCT` → keep as is; do not translate
- `Phụ lục I-1` → keep as is

### International standards and acronyms (keep in all documents)
- `EVFTA`, `CPTPP`, `RCEP`, `UKVFTA`, `WTO`, `FTA`
- `Incoterms`, `FOB`, `CIF`, `EXW`
- `ICC`, `UNCITRAL`, `VIAC` (keep alongside expanded form on first use)
- `HS Code`, `C/O` (Certificate of Origin)
- `CFS` (Certificate of Free Sale)
- `DICA` (Direct Investment Capital Account)
- `Apostille`
- `PCT`, `Madrid` (IP registration systems)

### Currency
- `VNĐ` / `VND` — keep the abbreviation, use Vietnamese format for amounts in VI and international format in EN:
  - VI: `1.000.000 VNĐ` (period as thousands separator)
  - EN: `VND 1,000,000` (comma as thousands separator)
